HTML Table Example

This table demonstrates the use of border, rowspan, and colspan attributes.

Student Math Science English
John 85 90 88
Jane 95 (Math & Science) 92
Mike 78 82 80
85 88 90

Explanation:

  • border="2" adds a 2-pixel border around the table.
  • colspan="2" merges two columns into one cell.
  • rowspan="2" merges two rows into one cell.
